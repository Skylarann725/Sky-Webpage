About Me


Welcome to my page! I am glad you found me! Let me start by telling you a little bit about myself. I graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2013 with a Bachelors Degree in Psychology and a Minor in Legal Studies. I am currently attending the UCF Coding Bootcamp. I know what you may be thinking. Psychology, Legal Studies, and Web Development are three completely different fields. Well, it took me some time to figure out what I wanted to be when I grew up but I have finally figured it out. I want to be a Web Developer. I absolutely love being able to design websites and applications from scratch and make them my own. I also love to learn. The field of technology is constantly changing which means that I will constantly be learning new and exciting things. I enjoy being challenged. It is exciting to me when I am finally able to overcome an obstacle that I encounter while working on a website or application. Feel free to explore some of the projects that I have worked on in my portfolio section!

In my spare time, I like to go boating. I enjoy wake-surfing and wake-boarding. I enjoy going to the beach. My other hobbies include reading and spending time with friends. Like any girl, I also enjoy shopping. I love to travel. So far, I have been to several countries, inlcuding France, England, and Germany. My dream is to be able to travel to every country in my lifetime. Scroll down to see some of my travel pictures!
